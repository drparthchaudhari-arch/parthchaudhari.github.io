Accessibility

Accessibility Plan

Statement of commitment and implementation targets

Commitment

VetLudics is committed to identifying, removing, and preventing accessibility barriers and to building pages that are usable with keyboard navigation, assistive technologies, and mobile touch interactions.

Current Priorities

  • Maintain text contrast at or above WCAG 2.1 AA thresholds.
  • Ensure interactive controls remain keyboard operable and reachable.
  • Improve image alt text coverage and semantic labeling across tools and study pages.

Feedback Mechanism

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, email parth@parthchaudhari.com with the page URL and issue description.

Target

Target conformance: WCAG 2.1 Level AA by December 31, 2026.
