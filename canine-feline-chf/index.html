Core Workflow

  1. Recognize cardiopulmonary failure signals and separate urgent from non-urgent presentations.
  2. Work up likely CHF with structured imaging, rhythm, perfusion, and laboratory context.
  3. Stage and phenotype the case to align monitoring intensity and treatment decisions.
  4. Adjust follow-up based on respiratory effort trends, recurrence risk, and owner adherence.
