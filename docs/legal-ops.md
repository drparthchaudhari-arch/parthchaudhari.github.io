# Legal Operations Checklist

Last reviewed: 2026-02-15

## Quarterly Compliance Reminder

1. Confirm educational disclaimer banner visibility on mobile and desktop.
2. Verify `/terms/`, `/privacy/`, `/licenses/`, and `/accessibility-plan/` links are live.
3. Re-test account data export and account deletion flows.
4. Re-check calculator acknowledgement gating on `/tools/*`.
5. Update clinical page last-reviewed dates where medical content changed.

## Manual Actions Outside Repository

1. Archive.org URL removal requests for deprecated case-study URLs.
2. Google Search Console URL removals for deleted legacy routes.
3. Maintain offline consent/insurance/legal records as required.
4. Review business and immigration legal status with licensed counsel when monetization changes.

## Change Log Practice

- Keep legal policy edits in dated Git commits.
- Include effective date updates in page content when policy language changes.
