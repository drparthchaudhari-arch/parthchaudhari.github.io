Editorial Policy

VetLudics publishes educational veterinary material for study and clinical reasoning practice. This page explains how content is written, reviewed, and corrected.

Last reviewed: February 13, 2026

Authorship and Credentials

Content is authored and maintained by Parth Chaudhari (BVSc&AH) with emphasis on NAVLE preparation, clinical pattern recognition, and educational clarity.

Source Standards

  • Primary references are veterinary textbooks, guidelines, and trusted clinical manuals.
  • Pages with clinical algorithms include source baselines where feasible.
  • Tools are for educational support and should be cross-checked against local protocols.

Last Reviewed Process

Key pages include a manual Last reviewed date. Review cycles prioritize high-impact pages such as emergency algorithms, dosing tools, and NAVLE study hubs.

Corrections and Feedback

If you identify an issue, share it through the contact page. Verified corrections are applied as quickly as possible and reflected in the next review timestamp.

Contact About and Credentials

Educational use only. Content is not personalized medical advice for a specific patient.
