About Me

Dedicated to Animal Health & Welfare

I am an internationally trained veterinarian with more than four years of clinical experience in large-animal medicine and current clinical practice experience in small-animal medicine in Canada. I am employed as a veterinary assistant and technician while completing the requirements for licensure in North America.

My clinical interests include internal medicine, emergency and critical care, and the application of evidence-based practice to improve patient outcomes. I am preparing for the North American Veterinary Licensing Examination (NAVLE) and remain committed to ongoing professional development.
