Core Workflow

  1. Identify airway, breathing, circulation threats before choosing a disease label.
  2. Stabilize oxygen delivery, perfusion, and immediate life-threatening derangements first.
  3. Use focused diagnostics to confirm priorities rather than delay treatment decisions.
  4. Reassess frequently and escalate if perfusion, mentation, or respiratory effort worsens.
