Privacy

Privacy Policy

This site is local-first. Optional sync is available if you choose to log in.

What Data We Collect

  • Email address (only if you opt in to account sync).
  • Learning progress data such as case completions, study plan state, and optional display name.
  • No payment data and no sensitive medical records are collected.

How Data Is Used

  • Data is used only to sync your progress across devices.
  • We do not use your email or progress data for marketing.
  • Anonymous mode remains available at all times.

How To Delete Your Data

  • Use the export function on Account before deleting if you want a backup.
  • Delete local browser data from your browser settings for anonymous mode data.
  • For synced data deletion requests, email contact@parthchaudhari.com.
  • If a direct delete-account button is added later, it will permanently remove synced account and progress records.

Contact

For privacy or account requests, contact contact@parthchaudhari.com.
