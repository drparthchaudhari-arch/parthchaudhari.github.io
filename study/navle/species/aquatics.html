Species-Wise Topic Separation

System Buckets for Aquatics

  • Water chemistry: ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, pH, dissolved oxygen, salinity, and gas supersaturation.
  • Infectious categories: bacterial, protozoal, fungal or water mold, and selected viral syndromes.
  • Parasitic disease: internal and external parasites with tank-management links.
  • Toxicity and trauma: chlorine/chloramine, metal toxicity, and handling-associated trauma.

How NAVLE Asks Aquatics

  • A tank system crash occurs with specific chemistry values. Which correction is highest priority first?
  • An aquatic group has progressive lesions and behavior changes. Which disease category is most likely?
  • A mixed-sign case asks whether husbandry, toxin, or infection is the dominant driver.
