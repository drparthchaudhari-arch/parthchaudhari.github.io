System Buckets for Porcine

  • Cardiovascular, GI, hemic/lymphatic, integumentary, musculoskeletal, nervous, respiratory, urinary, behavior, and multisystemic domains.
  • Herd respiratory and enteric outbreak patterns drive most management decisions.
  • Neurologic and toxic feed/water imbalance events are high-yield differential buckets.
  • Regulatory disease awareness and reporting logic are frequently tested.

How NAVLE Asks Porcine

  • A barn-level respiratory trend asks for first containment and sampling strategy.
  • A piglet enteric outbreak asks for immediate control and treatment priorities.
  • A food-animal medication stem asks for residue-aware and stewardship-safe choice.
