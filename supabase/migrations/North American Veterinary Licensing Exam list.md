This document addresses the species and diagnoses assessed on the North American Veterinary Licensing Examination® (NAVLE®). The species and diagnoses listed in the outline are examples. Test items may include diseases, symptoms, etc., that are not specifically noted in the outline. The NAVLE program continually reviews its exam to ensure the content remains relevant to veterinary practice. As practice guidelines change or are introduced, the content on NAVLE is reviewed and modified as necessary.
