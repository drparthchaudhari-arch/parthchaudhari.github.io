ER Algorithms Library (1-minute)

Use these rapid frameworks for the first minute, then expand into full diagnostics and definitive care.

Back to Tools Emergency Triage Hub

Critical Scenarios

GDV

First 60 sec: Oxygen, IV access x2, shock bolus aliquots, decompress stomach.

Next: Lactate, ECG, stabilization, surgery + gastropexy.

DKA

First 60 sec: Restore perfusion with isotonic fluids, evaluate K before insulin.

Next: CRI insulin, serial electrolytes, glucose trending, trigger search.

Status Epilepticus

First 60 sec: Diazepam/midazolam, airway/oxygen, glucose check.

Next: Levetiracetam or phenobarb load, temperature control, cause workup.

Anaphylaxis

First 60 sec: IM epinephrine, oxygen, IV access, fluid bolus.

Next: Antihistamine/steroid adjuncts, BP monitoring, repeat epi if needed.

Shock (Undifferentiated)

First 60 sec: Perfusion exam, POCUS if available, balanced bolus aliquots.

Next: Reassess mentation, pulse quality, lactate, urine output.

CHF Decompensation

First 60 sec: Minimize stress, oxygen, furosemide, point-of-care thoracic imaging.

Next: Nitroglycerin/pimobendan strategy, monitor RR effort and perfusion.

Hyperkalemia

First 60 sec: ECG, calcium gluconate for membrane stabilization.

Next: Insulin + dextrose, fluid support, remove underlying source.

Urethral Obstruction (Cat)

First 60 sec: Stabilize, treat hyperK risk, analgesia and decompression planning.

Next: Relieve obstruction, correct electrolytes/acidosis, monitor post-obstructive diuresis.

Heatstroke

First 60 sec: Controlled cooling, oxygen, IV access and perfusion support.

Next: Stop cooling at ~39.4 C, coagulation monitoring, organ injury surveillance.

Sepsis

First 60 sec: Perfusion-guided fluids, broad-spectrum antimicrobials early.

Next: Source control, serial lactate/perfusion checks, vasopressors as needed.

Algorithm cards are for educational rapid recall and do not replace full critical care protocols.

Approved reference baseline (reviewed February 13, 2026): Drug label search (DailyMed), IRIS stages, Normal lab values, Heartworm treatment protocol, Lab test protocols, Microchip lookup, Vertebral Heart Score, BCS charts, ACVIM cardiology consensus guideline references, Dental charts, AAHA vaccination guidelines, Flea/tick product info, Dog/cat breed search, and RECOVER CPR guidelines.
